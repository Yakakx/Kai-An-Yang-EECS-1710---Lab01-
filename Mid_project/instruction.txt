Using the mouse to play the one-button game. Moving the mouse can control the direction of the rocket and pressing the mouse can make the rocket accelerate. The game has a 25-second time limit, and the way to get the score is to control the rocket to hit the circle which will appear randomly and disappears soon. When the time end, it will show the final score that the user gets. Also, the game has a gravity setting, which means if the user can't control the rocket well, the rocket will drop out of space.
